## Importing an Existing Wallet

### When to Use This

- You already have a RhizaCore wallet
- You're moving to a new device
- You want to add a second wallet

### Step 1: Start Import

1. Click **"Open Wallet"** on homepage
2. Click **"Import Wallet"**

### Step 2: Enter Your Phrase

**What You'll See:**
- 24 empty boxes numbered 1-24

**What to Do:**
1. Get your written paper with your 24 words
2. Type each word in order
3. Or click "Paste Sequence" if you have it copied

**Tips:**
- Words are all lowercase
- No punctuation needed
- System will auto-complete as you type

### Step 3: Set Password (Optional)

**If you had a password before:**
- Enter the same password

**If this is a new device:**
- You can set a new password
- Or leave blank (not recommended)

### Step 4: Complete Import

1. Click **"Authorize Vault Access"**
2. System checks your phrase
3. If correct, you're in!

**If It Fails:**
- Check each word carefully
- Make sure they're in the right order
- Verify spelling (no typos)

## Troubleshooting

### I Forgot My Password

**Solution:**
1. You can still access your wallet with your 24-word phrase
2. Go to "Import Wallet"
3. Enter your 24 words
4. Set a new password
5. Your wallet is restored!

**Important:** This only works if you have your 24-word phrase!

### I Lost My 24-Word Phrase

**Unfortunately:**
- If you lost your phrase AND forgot your password, your wallet cannot be recovered
- This is by design for security
- RhizaCore cannot help (we don't have your phrase)

**Prevention:**
- Always keep multiple copies of your phrase
- Store in different safe locations
- Consider a fireproof safe

### Transaction is Pending

**What to Do:**
1. Wait 1-2 minutes (usually resolves itself)
2. Check your internet connection
3. Check blockchain explorer for status
4. If stuck after 10 minutes, contact support

**Common Causes:**
- Network congestion
- Low fee (rare on TON)
- Internet connection issues

### Wrong Amount Sent

**Important:**
- Blockchain transactions cannot be reversed
- Once sent, it's permanent
- Always double-check before confirming

**Prevention:**
- Send a small test amount first
- Double-check the address
- Verify the amount before confirming

### Can't See My Balance

**Solutions:**
1. **Refresh** - Pull down to refresh
2. **Check Internet** - Make sure you're online
3. **Wait** - Sometimes takes a few seconds
4. **Restart App** - Close and reopen

### Wallet Not Loading

**Try These Steps:**
1. Check internet connection
2. Clear browser cache
3. Try different browser
4. Restart your device
5. Check if website is down (status page)

## Glossary

**Simple Terms:**

- **Wallet** - Like a digital bank account you control
- **TON** - The type of money (cryptocurrency) you use
- **Address** - Like your account number for receiving money
- **Transaction** - Sending or receiving money
- **Balance** - How much money you have

**Technical Terms:**

- **Mnemonic Phrase** - Your 24-word recovery phrase
- **Private Key** - Secret code that controls your wallet (derived from phrase)
- **Public Key** - Your wallet address (safe to share)
- **Blockchain** - The technology that records all transactions
- **Gas Fee** - Small fee to process transactions
- **Non-Custodial** - You control your keys, not a company
