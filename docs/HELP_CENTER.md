### What is a mnemonic phrase?

**Simple:** It's 24 special words that are like a master key to your wallet. If you lose them, you lose your money forever.

**Technical:** A BIP39 mnemonic phrase is a human-readable representation of your wallet's private key. It's generated using cryptographic randomness and can restore your wallet on any device.

**Example:**
```

guitar hammer island jungle keyboard lemon
mountain notebook ocean planet question rainbow
```

### What are network fees?

**Simple:** A tiny fee (usually $0.01-0.05) paid to the blockchain to process your transaction.

**Technical:** Gas fees compensate validators for processing and securing transactions on the TON network.

**Who Gets the Fee?**
- Not RhizaCore
- Goes to blockchain validators
- Keeps the network running

**Typical Fees:**
- Send TON: ~$0.01
- Send tokens: ~$0.02
- Swap tokens: ~$0.05

---

### Can I cancel a transaction?

**No.** Once a transaction is confirmed on the blockchain, it's permanent and cannot be reversed.

**Why?**
- Blockchain transactions are immutable
- This prevents fraud and double-spending
- It's a core feature of cryptocurrency

**Prevention:**
- Always double-check addresses
- Send small test amounts first
- Verify recipient before sending large amounts

---

- Slow: 30 seconds (rare)

**Factors Affecting Speed:**
- Network congestion
- Transaction fee
- Blockchain confirmation time

**If Stuck:**
- Wait 1-2 minutes
- Check blockchain explorer
- Contact support if stuck >10 minutes

### What is session timeout?

**Simple:** If you don't use your wallet for 15 minutes, it automatically logs you out for security.

**How It Works:**
1. You login to your wallet
2. System tracks your activity (mouse, keyboard, scroll)
3. After 13 minutes of inactivity, you see a warning
4. You have 2 minutes to click "Stay Logged In"
5. If you don't, you're logged out automatically

**Why?**
- Protects you if you forget to logout
- Prevents unauthorized access
- Security best practice

**Disable It?**
- No, it's always active for your protection
- But you can quickly login again

## Troubleshooting

**Solution:** Use your 24-word mnemonic phrase to restore your wallet.

**Steps:**
1. Go to "Import Wallet"
2. Enter all 24 words in order
3. Set a new password
4. Your wallet is restored with all funds intact

**If you also lost your mnemonic:**
- Unfortunately, your wallet cannot be recovered
- This is by design for security
- Always backup your mnemonic phrase!

---

### I lost my 24-word phrase

**If you still have your password:**
1. Login to your wallet
2. Go to Settings â†’ Wallet Manager
3. Export your wallet backup
4. The backup contains your mnemonic (encrypted)
5. Store it safely this time!

- Your wallet cannot be recovered

- RhizaCore cannot help (we don't have your keys)

**Prevention:**
- Write your phrase on paper
- Store in multiple safe locations
- Make multiple copies
- Use a fireproof safe

### I sent to wrong address

**Unfortunately:** Blockchain transactions cannot be reversed.

**What Happened:**
- Your funds went to the wrong address
- They're now controlled by whoever has that address's private key
- RhizaCore cannot retrieve them

**Prevention:**
- Always double-check addresses
- Send small test amounts first
- Use address book for frequent recipients
- Scan QR codes instead of typing

**Possible Recovery:**
- If you know the recipient, ask them to send back
- If it's your own address, you can access it
- If it's a random address, funds are likely lost

### What is staking?

**Simple:** Lock up your $RZC coins to earn more coins over time. Like a savings account that pays interest.

**How It Works:**

2. Your coins help secure the network

**Risks:**

- Smart contract risk (rare)
