# How to Complete the Influencer Collaboration Task - User Guide

## 🎯 Task Overview
- **Reward**: 1,000 RZC (highest in the system!)
- **Difficulty**: Expert level
- **Time Limit**: 30 days
- **Requirement**: Get a crypto influencer (1K+ followers) to mention RhizaCore

## 📋 Step-by-Step Guide

### Step 1: Find Suitable Influencers

#### Minimum Requirements
- **1,000+ followers** on any supported platform
- **Active in crypto/blockchain space**
- **Engaged audience** (not just follower count)
- **Authentic account** (real followers, not bots)

#### Recommended Platforms
1. **Twitter/X** - Crypto analysts, traders, news accounts
2. **YouTube** - Crypto channels, educational content creators
3. **Instagram** - Crypto educators, lifestyle influencers in crypto
4. **TikTok** - Crypto content creators, young audience focus

#### How to Find Influencers
```
🔍 Search Methods:
• Twitter: Search hashtags #crypto #blockchain #DeFi
• YouTube: Search "crypto review" "blockchain analysis"
• Instagram: Look for #cryptocurrency #cryptotrading
• TikTok: Search #crypto #cryptotok #blockchain

🔍 Tools to Use:
• Social Blade (check follower growth)
• Twitter Analytics (engagement rates)
• YouTube Creator Stats
• Instagram Insights (if available)
```

### Step 2: Research and Prepare

#### Before Reaching Out
1. **Study their content** - Understand their style and audience
2. **Check engagement** - Look for active comments and interactions
3. **Verify authenticity** - Ensure followers are real and engaged
4. **Note their rates** - Many influencers have published rates

#### Prepare Your Pitch
```
📝 Include in Your Outreach:
• Brief introduction of yourself
• Clear explanation of RhizaCore project
• Specific collaboration proposal
• Compensation offer (if any)
• Timeline and deliverables
• Contact information
```

### Step 3: Outreach and Negotiation

#### Sample Outreach Message
```
Subject: Collaboration Opportunity - RhizaCore Project

Hi [Influencer Name],

I'm reaching out regarding a collaboration opportunity with RhizaCore, 
an innovative blockchain project focused on [brief description].

We're looking for authentic crypto influencers to help spread awareness 
about our RZC token and ecosystem. Your content on [specific topic] 
really resonates with our target audience.

Collaboration Options:
• Sponsored post/video about RhizaCore
• Review of our platform features
• Educational content about RZC utility
• Interview or AMA session

We can offer [compensation/value exchange] for quality content that 
introduces RhizaCore to your audience.

Would you be interested in learning more?

Best regards,
[Your name]
```

#### Negotiation Tips
- Be transparent about the project and your goals
- Offer fair compensation for their time and reach
- Be flexible on content format and style
- Respect their creative process and audience

### Step 4: Content Creation and Publication

#### Content Requirements
The influencer's content should:
- **Mention "RhizaCore" or "RZC"** clearly
- **Be positive or neutral** in tone
- **Reach their actual audience** (not hidden/private)
- **Be published within 30 days** of task start

#### Content Examples
```
✅ Good Examples:
• "Just learned about @RhizaCore and their RZC token..."
• "Reviewing RhizaCore's innovative approach to..."
• "RZC has some interesting utility features..."
• Video explaining RhizaCore's value proposition

❌ Avoid:
• Misleading claims about guaranteed returns
• Pump-and-dump style promotion
• Hidden or private content
• Content that violates platform rules
```

### Step 5: Document Everything

#### Required Proof Documentation
1. **Influencer Verification**
   - Screenshot showing follower count
   - Profile screenshot with verification badges
   - Evidence of crypto/blockchain focus

2. **Content Evidence**
   - Screenshots of the published content
   - Direct links to posts/videos
   - Timestamp showing publication date
   - Engagement metrics (likes, comments, shares)

3. **Collaboration Proof**
   - Screenshots of your communication
   - Agreement or confirmation messages
   - Payment receipts (if applicable)
   - Any contracts or terms discussed

#### File Organization Tips
```
📁 Create a folder with:
├── influencer_profile_proof.png

├── content_screenshot_2.png
├── follower_count_proof.png
├── communication_evidence.pdf
├── content_links.txt

```

#### Using the Verification Modal
1. Click "Collaborate" on the Influencer Collaboration task

3. Fill out the description field with detailed information:


4. Upload all proof files (images, PDFs, documents)
5. Submit for admin review

## 💡 Pro Tips for Success

### Finding the Right Influencers
1. **Micro-influencers** (1K-10K) often have higher engagement
2. **Niche relevance** matters more than follower count
3. **Check recent posts** for engagement quality
4. **Look for authentic interactions** in comments

### Negotiation Strategies
1. **Start with smaller influencers** to build experience
2. **Offer value beyond money** (early access, exclusive info)
3. **Be professional** but personable in communication
4. **Respect their content style** and audience

### Documentation Best Practices
1. **Screenshot everything** before it can be deleted
2. **Save original URLs** and archive content
3. **Document the timeline** of all interactions
4. **Keep communication records** organized

### Common Mistakes to Avoid
❌ **Don't**: Contact influencers without researching their content  
❌ **Don't**: Make unrealistic promises about RZC returns  
❌ **Don't**: Submit incomplete or unclear proof  
❌ **Don't**: Wait until the last minute (30-day limit)  
❌ **Don't**: Try to fake collaborations or use fake influencers  

## 🚨 Important Warnings

### Compliance Requirements
- Follow all platform terms of service
- Ensure influencers disclose sponsored content appropriately
- Don't make financial advice claims
- Respect advertising regulations in your jurisdiction

### Quality Standards
- Admins will reject low-quality or suspicious submissions
- Fake collaborations will result in task failure
- Misleading proof will be flagged
- Multiple rejections may affect future task eligibility
