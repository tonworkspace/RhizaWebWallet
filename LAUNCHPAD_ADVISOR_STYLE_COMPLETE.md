## Copywriting Principles

### **1. Clear & Concise**
- Short sentences
- Bullet points
- Scannable content

### **2. Benefit-Focused**
- "You get..." not "We offer..."
- Outcome-oriented
- User-centric language

### **3. Action-Oriented**
- Strong CTAs
- Imperative verbs
- Clear next steps

### **4. Transparent**
- Honest about risks
- Clear disclaimers
- No hype or exaggeration
